Atlas Partner Portal — Support and Security Policy
Policy ID: ATL-OPS-004
Effective date: October 1, 2026
Policy owner: Daniel Okafor, Head of Platform Operations

Support coverage

Standard support is available Monday through Friday from 08:00 to 18:00 UK time, excluding public holidays in England and Wales. Severity-one incidents are monitored and handled 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Incident priorities and response targets

Severity one means the production service is unavailable, data integrity is at immediate risk, or a confirmed security incident affects customer data. Operations must acknowledge a severity-one incident within 30 minutes and provide status updates at least every 60 minutes.

Severity two means a major workflow is unavailable but an acceptable workaround exists. Operations must acknowledge a severity-two incident within four business hours.

Severity three covers minor defects and general support questions. Operations must acknowledge a severity-three request within two business days.

Security controls

All production traffic must use TLS 1.2 or later. Production data must be encrypted at rest using provider-managed keys. Administrative access requires multi-factor authentication and is reviewed every quarter. Access is granted according to least-privilege principles and must be removed within one business day after a user no longer requires it.

Data location and retention

Customer production data and backups must remain in the European Union. Uploaded study documents are retained for seven years unless a customer contract or legal hold requires a longer period. Application logs are retained for 90 days. Security audit records are retained for two years.

Backups and recovery

Production data is backed up every six hours. Backups are encrypted and tested through a restore exercise at least once every quarter. The recovery time objective is four hours, and the recovery point objective is six hours.

Security incident communication

The security lead must notify the product owner and legal counsel immediately after confirming a customer-data incident. Affected customers must receive an initial notification within 24 hours of confirmation unless law enforcement or legal counsel requires a different timeline.

Change management

Production changes require a reviewed pull request, successful automated checks, and an approved deployment record. Emergency changes may use an expedited approval, but they must be reviewed retrospectively within two business days.
